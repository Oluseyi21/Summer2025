A Legendary Camera

First launched in 1977, the Polaroid OneStep was a game-changer in photography. It allowed people to take a photo and print it instantly- no film development needed. With its colorful design and simple one-button operation, it became the best-selling camera of its time and a true icon of the 70s and 80s.
